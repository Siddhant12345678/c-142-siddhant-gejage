AI PING PONG










Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight.

  2. Make yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

  3. Move your right wrist in ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.

  4. Now click the play button, and again make yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.

  5. Now the red paddel is in your paddel.
